EVENTS

CACM believes in nurthuring students with skills and sharing knowledge to achieve and succeed. ACM organizes regular events and conferences each year to celebrate ACM's spirit and accomplishments in computing. These events are designed to support and promote the field of computing. These events include several competitions, workshops, industrial visits, hackathons and conferences. The series of events organised by Chitkara ACM students Chapter goes on and we never stops learning here.
